Mission Statement

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the connection between art and technology. The museum aims to inspire creativity, curiosity, and innovation through interactive exhibits and digital art experiences.

Milestones:

  • 2018: Museum founded
  • 2019: First digital interactive exhibition launched
  • 2022: Expanded gallery space
  • 2025: Over 100,000 annual visitors
